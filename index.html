About Our Arcade

Welcome to Gameland! We will be opening our amazing arcade in Rochester, Minnesota in the summer of 2017. Here we strive to incite curiousity in every mind by offering fun for everyone. We hope to one day have a location in every state of the United States so that every kid can have access to our one-of-a-kind arcade.


We offer many activites to ensure that you, your family, and your friends never stop having fun! We are proud to introduce our world-renowned snack bar where our innovative chef offers signature appetizers from countries all around the world. In addition to our snack bar we also have a dessert bar where we import pastries from France and we serve it with our secret, homemade recipe for icecream. Together, you will experience the tastiest meal of your life! Once you taste our chef's creations you will never want to leave!


We also have an endless amount of games for children to play in the arcade. This includes any game ranging from skee-ball all the way to virtual reality. Our breakthrough technology makes us one the of the most unique arcades in the world! Not only will you have an astounding time here but you will want to come back everyday! To top it all off we have a fireworks display every night to ensure that everyones night was memorable. If you leave our arcade less than 100% satisfied, we will give you your money back.
