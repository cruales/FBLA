Who We Are

Christie Ruales

CEO & Founder

Christie Ruales, at just 17, founded the company in 2016. Since then she has opened 3 branches in the United States. These locations include Florida, California, and Massachusetts. Her expertise in the field of computer science has allowed her to create the innnovative technology that makes Gameland so unique. Her vision for the company is to open branches in every state of the United States so that everyone, adults and kids alike, will have access to a fun and relaxing environment whenever they choose.
